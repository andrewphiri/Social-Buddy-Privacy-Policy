# Social-Buddy-Privacy-Policy

Last Updated: 01/01/2026

Social Buddy ("we", "our", or "the App") is a personal conversation-assistant application designed to help users improve texting, generate responses, and manage social interactions more effectively. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect
1.1. Information You Provide Voluntarily

We may collect the following information when you use the App:

Text you input (messages, prompts, generated responses)

Optional contact/message content you choose to paste or type into the app

We do NOT read or access your device messages, contacts, or social accounts.
All text is entered manually by you.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

To help improve performance and maintain the app, we may collect:

Device model

Operating system version

App version

Basic analytics (screens opened, features used)

No personal messages or private data are collected automatically.

1.3. Subscription & Billing (Optional)

If you subscribe to premium features:

Google Play may provide us with non-personal purchase tokens

We do not receive or store your payment card details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

Generate message suggestions

Improve your results and tone customizations

Save templates or favorites you choose to store

Improve app performance and user experience

Provide customer support when needed

We never:

Sell your data

Use your content for advertising

Track you across other apps or services

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Text you enter is processed on-device or securely transmitted to AI APIs (e.g., Google AI via Gemini or similar services), depending on the feature.

Generated messages and saved templates are stored locally on your device unless cloud sync is added in the future (and you opt in).

Analytics is anonymized and cannot identify you personally.

We only share information in these limited cases:

4.1. With Service Providers

Such as AI model APIs for generating responses.

4.2. Legal Requirements

Only if required by law.

We do NOT share your data with advertisers or third parties for marketing.
